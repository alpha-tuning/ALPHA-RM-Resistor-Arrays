# TRADEMARKS AND BRANDING

**ALPHA** and the **ALPHA logo** are brand identifiers associated with this project.

The open-source design files in this repository may allow use, modification, manufacture, and redistribution of the hardware under the applicable license terms, but that does **not** grant permission to use the ALPHA name, ALPHA logo, or ALPHA branding in a way that suggests endorsement, affiliation, or origin unless explicitly authorized.

## Allowed

You may use the name ALPHA in a factual way to provide attribution, such as:

- Derived from ALPHA open-source hardware
- Based on ALPHA design files
- Original source: ALPHA

## Not allowed without permission

You may not:

- Rebrand your product as official ALPHA hardware if it is not
- Use the ALPHA logo as your own product branding
- Imply endorsement, partnership, or certification by ALPHA when none exists
- Remove attribution and present ALPHA-origin work as entirely original

## Derivative products

If you manufacture or sell derivative boards, you should use your own branding while preserving attribution to ALPHA as the original source.

Example:

**YourBrand RM Network Board — derived from ALPHA open-source hardware**
